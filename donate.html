A mere contribution of at least Rs. 1000 (INR) will help a type-one diabetic child afford and access life-giving insulin for an entire month.Every donation makes a tremendous difference!Type-one diabetic children in India do not have the access to quality healthcare and sufficient resources like glucose monitors, insulin cartridges, insulin pens and CGMs.An average type-one diabetic spends over 20,000 Indian National Rupees on their diabetes every single year.From purchasing insulin, syringes, lancets to diagnostic tests and transport; 20,000 rupees is an inconceivable amount to spend for presumably a daily income worker. Let alone affordability of the essential resources, their mental health is ever deteriorating.From not being able to afford a refrigerator to store life-giving insulin, to missing school due to lack of awareness; the underprivileged t-1 diabetics are facing various problems. Your donation will go a long way in supporting these children! Donate here

All donations go to sponsoring life saving insulin

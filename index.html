GET TO KNOW US!

Speak Sprouts is an organization that teaches Speech and Debate skills to 4th and 5th graders around Southeastern PA.

Our Mission

To empower young students by providing an interactive, novel platform that fosters the growth of their public speaking and reading literacy skills, setting them up to be successful leaders.
